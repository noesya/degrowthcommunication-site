This website aims to make degrowth knowledge available at your fingertips while providing advice on using it effectively and with empathy.

But why is this important? 

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: alt_background text: >-

Despite 2023 marking the hottest year on record, corporate broadcast news decreased climate coverage by 25 %

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: image title: >- ranks: self: false data: text: >- image: id: "47641476-8dd8-4737-8f7f-f7c62a69dbb4" file: "47641476-8dd8-4737-8f7f-f7c62a69dbb4" alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: alt_background text: >-

Clearly, media owned by banks and billionaires (who have vested interests in maintaining the status quo) will not help us.

So we need to improve our strategies. 

We often talk about 'degrees of warming,' but 99 % of people don't really know what that means. It's important to explain clearly what it actually leads to. It's not just about hotter weather or more rain.

We need to start talking about the severe consequences that are headed our way if we maintain business as usual: Wars, Global Financial Collapse, Famine, Diseases, Hyper Violence, and Mass Death.

Trying to avoid scaring people by not talking about these things has been a huge mistake.

But we must not fall into despair. We have a responsibility to act.

Degrowth can offer a narrative filled with hope and strength.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

In these critical times, our collective action is more than a choice—it's a necessity. The challenges we face are daunting, but within them lies the opportunity for profound change. 

Let this moment galvanise us. Each effort, each voice, and each step forward contributes to a tide of positive change. You are a vital part of this movement.

Embrace the responsibility with courage and determination. It’s time to act, not just for ourselves but for all the beings on this planet and the generations to come.

Stand up, speak out, and let's create a future where everyone thrives.

Your action today is the hope for our tomorrow.
