"We focus on degrees of warming, which means literally nothing to 99 % of the public. What that actually means needs to be laid out, and it should not just be heat waves or more rain, which also doesn't sound impactful.

The war, global financial collapse, famine, disease, hyper-violence and mass death need to be laid out explicitly. This constant emphasis on not overwhelming or frightening people has been one of the biggest mistakes in human history."
