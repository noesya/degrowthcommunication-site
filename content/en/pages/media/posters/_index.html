A group of students from the University of Amsterdam, in collaboration with Ontgroei made some truly beautiful degrowth posters and slogans.

These impactful posters are designed to raise awareness about the importance of embracing degrowth principles for a sustainable future.

The QR code takes you to a LinkTree with a list of helpful degrowth websites.

These amazing posters were created by Degrowth Copenhagen, an international forum of individuals who share degrowth values and a degrowth vision.

These posters were made for a poster storm action which aimed to raise awareness about degrowth. These posters are meant to highlight unconventional or non-mainstream policies/actions that are true and powerful climate solutions

The QR code takes you to the Instagram posts for each poster so you can learn more about each one in the captions. Be sure to give them a follow!
